Our Mission & Vision

Empowering Youth, Transforming Lives

Mission

To empower young minds through quality education, skill development and holistic support,enabling them to become confident,independent and responsible citizens.

Vision

To be a leading foundation that creates sustainable impact by transforming lives and building a skilled, empowered and equitable society.

Our Founder

Brigadier (Dr.) Kishor Jadhav

Founder & Visionary Leader

A retired army officer with a heart for nation-building and youth empowerment. His vision and dedication laid the foundation of Anish Jadhav Memorial Foundation to create opportunities for those who need it the most.

Education creates opportunities and opportunities change lives.

Our Strategic Partner

In 2019, Anish Jadhav Memorial Foundation partnered with the Anudip Foundation to address the employability crisis directly. Our goal was to bridge the gap between education and employment for those who needed it most.

200+

Underprivileged Youths Trained

This initiative resulted in successful placements in the ITeS (Information Technology Enabled Services) and BFSI (Banking, Financial Services and Insurance) sectors in Pune.
