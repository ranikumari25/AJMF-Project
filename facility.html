Our Facilities

Every aspect of our campus is designed to support student success, from academics to wellness.

Training & Conference

8 state-of-the-art training rooms equipped with projectors and audio systems, plus a mini auditorium and conference hall for large gatherings and workshops.

Student Accommodation

Safe and comfortable residential dormitories capable of housing 150 students, along with dedicated separate accommodation for 10 full-time instructors.

Kitchen & Dining

A hygienic, fully functional industrial-grade kitchen and a spacious community dining hall serving nutritious, balanced meals three times a day.

Admin & Learning Center

A central hub containing the reception, administrative offices, staff rooms, and a well-stocked library for self-paced learning.

Eco-Friendly Infra

Committed to sustainability with a Sewage Treatment Plant (STP), Solar Power generation, Rainwater Harvesting, and energy-efficient architecture.

Digital Infrastructure

High-speed internet access across campus, provision of laptops for every student, and a dedicated computer lab for technical training.

Sports & Recreation

A large outdoor playground for cricket, volleyball, and morning yoga sessions to ensure physical fitness alongside mental growth.
